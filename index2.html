ABOUT HEXEIA

Founded in 2015, HEXEIA stands at the forefront of space technology innovation, specializing in the design and manufacturing of cutting-edge satellite components and systems. Our team of aerospace engineers, software developers, and materials scientists work collaboratively to push the boundaries of what's possible in orbital technology.

What sets HEXEIA apart is our integrated approach to space technology. While many companies focus on isolated components, we develop comprehensive solutions that work seamlessly together—from the satellite bus that forms the foundation of spacecraft to the sophisticated ADCS systems that maintain precise orientation in orbit. Our proprietary VHF/UHF communication cards ensure reliable data transmission across vast distances.

With a commitment to miniaturization without compromising functionality, HEXEIA's CubeSat and MicroSat platforms are enabling more organizations to access space than ever before. Whether supporting Earth observation missions, telecommunications, or scientific research, our technology is designed to perform reliably in the harshest environment known to humanity.
