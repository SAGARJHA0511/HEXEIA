About Hexeia

Hexeia is a space tech company specializing in end-to-end CubeSat development.

From advanced research and design to manufacturing, integration, testing, and launch services,

we provide innovative, cost-effective solutions for academic, commercial, and defense applications.

Our expertise includes high-resolution imaging, hyperspectral sensing, synthetic aperture radar,

and real-time data analytics.

We empower clients to harness space-based insights for environmental monitoring,

communications, and security.
